Detailed Description

Type "2 Blocs Vertical" feature. 

For white:
1        2
+-------+
|  [W]  |
3+-------4
|   B   |
+-------+
For black:
+-------+
1|   W   2
+-------+
|  [B]  |
3+-------4

(W = White, B = Black) The formula is: 4+1 - (2+3)
